Saint Seiya

This page is about Saint Seiya, a story that combines mythology, loyalty, and personal sacrifice. I chose this theme because it is meaningful to me and it is easy to build a purpose-driven page using only HTML.

Main idea

Saint Seiya focuses on fighters who protect Athena and face conflicts that test their values. The series explores identity, discipline, friendship, and endurance under pressure.

Why I like it

  • Mythology and symbols used to build the world
  • Characters with strong motivations and clear codes of honor
  • Long arcs that reward persistence and attention to detail
